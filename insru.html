Fully baked and ready to eat!

or enjoy them toasty warm!

Remove from pouch before heating.

For Toaster

  1. Warm pastry in toaster at lowest heat setting
  2. Cool briefly before handling

For Microwave

  1. Place pastry on microwave safe plate
  2. Heat on high for 3 seconds
  3. Cool brieftly before handling

Caution: If pastry is overheated, frosting/filling can become extremely hot and could cause burns. Due to possible risk of fire, never leave appliance unattended while in use. Children should be supervised. Appliances vary. Be sure your appliance is clean and funtioning correctly. Times given area approximate.
