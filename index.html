POP TARTS®

Frosted Chocolate Chip

Naturally & Artifically Flavored

Kellog's® Pop Tarts® chocolate chip flavored pasteries are a delicious way to start your day! Each toaster pastry contains gooey chocolate chips baked into a cookie crust that's drizzled with chocolatey icing and filled with chocolate flavored goodness. Goes great with dairy products! Grab a tall glass of milk and dunk away!

Each standard box of Pop Tarts® contains 8 Toaster Pastries Two pasteries per package. One toaster pastry per serving. Each box of Pop Tarts® has a net weight of 14.7 oz.

Each frosted chocolate chip Pop Tart® contains 210 calories per serving. One pastery contrains 2g saturated fat per serving and 250mg of sodium per serving, 10% of your daily value. Each Pop Tart® Toaster Pastry contains 17 grams of sugar.
